Sea Turtle Conservation

Every year thousands of hatchling turtles reach the ocean from their nests along the Malaysia's coast but only an estimated 1 out of 1,000 to 10,000 will survive to adulthood.

Coral Reef Restoration

As one of the most biologically diverse ecosystems on earth, more coral reefs are added to a threatened list that we can have a massive coral reef loss within the next few years.

Environmental Programme

Plastic is severely threatening the oceans. It is important for us to take action, and preserve the natural resources as well as its environmental richness.

Scientific Research

The methods of science include careful observation, record keeping, logical and mathematical reasoning, experimentation. Collaborators who are passionate, results oriented, love wildlife and enjoy working with the local communities are always welcomed.
